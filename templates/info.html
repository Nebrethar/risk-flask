Information


Thank you for your interest in this study.

Several projects have been assembled for this dashboard
for the purpose of this study.

In the repository screens, you may find two main types of content:
CII Best Practices data, and DoSOCSv2 license information

CII Best Practices can determine how well a company is following standard practices for good development and code.

DoSOCSv2 license scans provide license-related data that can be used to explore the project's
license compliance, as well as many other uses.

Both of these are available for you to explore as much as you would like.
Please refer to the study coordinator when you are finished with the dashboard.

Please click any of the above buttons when you would like to begin.

You are allowed as much time as you would like to view the dashboard.
